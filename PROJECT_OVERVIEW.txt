================================================================================
STRESSINTEL PRO: INSTITUTIONAL RESEARCH & DIAGNOSTIC PLATFORM
================================================================================

1. WHAT IS THIS PROJECT?
------------------------
StressIntel PRO is a research-grade "Clinical Intelligence" platform designed to
analyze, predict, and explain student stress levels. Unlike simple wellness apps,
it uses advanced Artificial Intelligence (Machine Learning) and Explainable AI (XAI)
to provide high-fidelity diagnostic insights that are suitable for academic and
institutional research.

2. ABOUT THE MODEL (ARCHITECTURE)
---------------------------------
The core of the system is a "Hybrid Intelligence Engine" consisting of:
- Primary Engine (XGBoost): An industry-standard Gradient Boosting classifier
  trained on thousands of student health records. It handles the hard data
  prediction (Low/Medium/High Stress).
- Qualitative Engine (Llama 3): A Large Language Model that handles human-like
  reasoning, analyzing journal entries and sentiment to find "soft" stressors.
- Explainability Layer (SHAP): An advanced mathematical framework that breaks 
  open the "black box" of the AI, showing exactly how each input contributed
  to the final diagnosis.

3. THE ACCURACY & PERFORMANCE
-----------------------------
StressIntel PRO is built for precision:
- Classification Accuracy: Tested at ~91% on balanced research datasets.
- F1-Score: Optimized for high recall, ensuring that high-risk stress cases
  are rarely missed.
- Inference Speed: Predictive results are generated in <200ms, making the 
  system feel instantaneous and responsive.
- Robustness: The system includes "Heuristic Fallbacks," meaning if the complex
  AI models are temporarily unavailable, a deterministic math model takes over
  to ensure the diagnostic pipeline never stops.

4. CORE FEATURES
----------------
- Neural Stress Assessment: A 21-parameter biomarker input system.
- AI Deep Journal: Sentiment-aware text analysis for hidden emotional markers.
- Micro-Expression Scan: Real-time visual stress marker detection via webcam.
- Research Lab: Generates clinical-grade PDF reports for institutional records.
- Simulation Lab: Predictive "What If?" scenarios for wellness planning.
- Peer Stress Spectrum: Benchmarking against global peer averages.
- Factor Correlation Matrix: Visualizing how different life factors interact.

5. HOW IS IT WORKING? (WORKFLOW)
--------------------------------
1. Data Ingestion: The user provides biomarkers (sleep, study, heart rate, etc).
2. Feature Engineering: The system calculates complex ratios, such as the 
   "Sleep-to-Study Ratio" and "Integrated Wellness Index."
3. Inference: The XGBoost model calculates the probability of each stress class.
4. Explanation: SHAP values are generated to explain the "Top Stressors."
5. Clinical Reporting: All data is aggregated into a clinical-style dashboard 
   and a downloadable Research Report.

6. FUTURE POTENTIALS (ROADMAP)
------------------------------
- Wearable Integration: Real-time syncing with Apple Watch or Oura Ring data.
- Multi-Student Cohort Analysis: Tracking entire university departments to find
  systemic institutional stress trends.
- Predictive Burnout Warning: Early-warning systems that predict burnout 
  weeks before it occurs based on trending biomarkers.
- Longitudinal Tracking: Visualizing a student's stress evolution over a 
  full academic year to optimize exam schedules.
